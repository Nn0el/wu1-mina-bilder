• Hem Om

Ingredients: 4 packs of ramen noodles 4 cups of vegetable broth 2 cups of water 1 tablespoon of soy sauce 1 tablespoon of miso paste 1 tablespoon of ginger, minced 1 tablespoon of garlic, minced 1 tablespoon of sesame oil 1 medium onion, sliced 4 oz of shiitake mushrooms, sliced 1 medium carrots, julienned 1 medium bok choy, chopped 1 package of firm tofu, drained and cut into cubes Vegetable oil for frying Toppings of your choice: green onions, sesame seeds, nori, etc. Instructions: In a large pot, heat the vegetable broth, water, soy sauce, miso paste, ginger, and garlic over medium heat. Let it simmer for 10 minutes. In a separate pan, heat a small amount of vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the tofu cubes and fry until golden brown. Remove from heat and set aside. In the same pan, add sesame oil and onion, and sauté until the onion is soft and translucent. Add the shiitake mushrooms and carrots and cook until the mushrooms have released their liquid. Add the bok choy to the broth and let it cook until wilted. Cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions. Divide the noodles among four bowls, and ladle the broth and vegetables over the noodles. Top with the fried tofu and any other toppings you prefer. Serve hot.
